Prioritized Action Plan

Quick Wins — Do This Week

1. Add Google Analytics (GA4) + Search Console
Critical
Install GA4 tracking code. Register site in Google Search Console. Without this, you're making decisions with zero data.
Effort: 1-2 hours Impact: Foundational — enables everything else
2. Add robots.txt + XML sitemap
Critical
Create a robots.txt allowing crawling and a sitemap.xml listing all URLs. Submit sitemap in Search Console. This is the #1 barrier to indexing.
Effort: 30 minutes Impact: Enables Google to find and index your site
3. Add Open Graph + Twitter Card meta tags
Critical
Add og:title, og:description, og:image, og:url + Twitter card tags. Every time someone shares your link on Instagram, Facebook, or Telegram, it will display with a beautiful preview instead of a blank box.
Effort: 30 minutes Impact: Dramatically better social sharing appearance
4. Add canonical URL tag
High
Add <link rel="canonical" href="https://doctorpidnebesna.com/"> to prevent duplicate content issues.
Effort: 5 minutes Impact: Clean SEO signal
5. Add structured data (JSON-LD)
High
Add FAQPage schema (for your FAQ section), Person schema (for Dr. Lyuba), and MedicalBusiness schema. FAQPage schema can show your Q&As directly in Google search results.
Effort: 1-2 hours Impact: Rich results in Google, builds E-E-A-T
6. Fix mobile performance (LCP 8.5s)
High
Optimize hero images (compress, use WebP), add lazy loading to below-fold images, and preload critical resources. Target LCP under 2.5s on mobile.
Effort: 2-4 hours Impact: Better Google ranking + lower bounce rate on mobile
7. Install Facebook/Meta Pixel
High
Even before running ads, the pixel starts building an audience of your visitors. When you're ready for ads, you'll have data to create lookalike audiences.
Effort: 30 minutes Impact: Future retargeting capability

Strategic Investments — This Quarter

8. Expand to multi-page site with blog
Critical
Create dedicated pages: /about, /services, /blog, /faq, /contact. Then start publishing 2-4 blog posts per month based on your "Myth vs. Science" format. Each post targets a specific keyword from the opportunities table.
Effort: 1-2 weeks for site structure, ongoing for content Impact: Organic traffic pipeline — potentially 500-2000 monthly visitors within 6 months
9. Create a lead magnet + email capture
Critical
Create a free PDF guide (e.g., "Чекліст домашньої аптечки для малюка" or "10 медичних міфів які шкодять дітям"). Add an email signup form to capture leads. Use a tool like MailerLite (free tier) for email automation.
Effort: 1-2 days Impact: Build owned audience. Email converts 3-5x better than social media.
10. Add privacy policy + cookie consent
High
Required for legal compliance (especially for medical services), Google Ads eligibility, and Facebook Ads. Use a template or generator for Ukrainian medical practice.
Effort: 2-3 hours Impact: Legal compliance + ad platform eligibility
11. Claim Google Business Profile
High
Even as an online practice, GBP helps you appear in "family doctor" and "pediatrician" searches. Add photos, services, operating hours, and encourage reviews.
Effort: 1-2 hours Impact: Local search visibility
12. Add video content
Medium
Short 60-90 second myth-busting videos (Reels/TikTok format) embedded on site and shared on social. Video builds trust faster than text for medical professionals. Even a simple "talking head" format works.
Effort: Ongoing (1-2 videos/week) Impact: Trust building + viral potential + YouTube SEO
13. Add more social proof & trust signals
Medium
Display diploma/certificate images, add more testimonials with names and photos (with consent), show "consultation counter" (e.g., "5000+ consultations"), and add response time indicator.
Effort: 1 day Impact: Higher conversion rate

Medium
Add a contact form, a booking calendar (Calendly-style), a WhatsApp/Viber option, and an email address. Some users are uncomfortable starting medical conversations on Telegram.
